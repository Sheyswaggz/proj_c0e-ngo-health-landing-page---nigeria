About Our Organization

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Health for All Nigeria emerged from a deep commitment to address healthcare disparities in underserved Nigerian communities. What began as a small grassroots initiative in Lagos has grown into a nationwide movement, reaching thousands of families across urban and rural areas. Our journey has been marked by dedication, compassion, and an unwavering belief that quality healthcare is a fundamental human right for every Nigerian, regardless of their location or economic status.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, culturally sensitive healthcare information and resources to underserved communities across Nigeria, empowering individuals with the knowledge and tools they need to make informed health decisions and improve their overall wellbeing.

Our Vision

A Nigeria where every individual, regardless of their geographic location or socioeconomic background, has access to quality healthcare information and services, leading to healthier communities and a stronger nation.
